Peanut Butter and Jelly Sandwich

Description

A timeless classic involving two slices of bread, jam, and none other than some fresh peanut butter. Why choose this? Well.. might I ask why not choose this beast of a meal. With protein, processed sugar, bleached flour, and love; this sandwich is truly a modern marvel.

Ingredients

  • Wonder bread
  • Peanut butter
  • Jelly

Assembly

  1. Gather all the ingredients together
  2. Open the bread
  3. Remove two slices, place aside
  4. Get the peanut butter
  5. Grab a knife from the drawer
  6. Open peanut butter lid
  7. Insert knife into open peanut butter jar and scoop
  8. Spread peanut butter on one slice of the bread
  9. Set aside newly-peanut-buttered bread
  10. Close the peanut butter with the lid
  11. Clean the knife in the sink, and dry
  12. Repeat steps 6-11 above, substituting jelly for peanut butter and use the other slice of bread that isn't smeared
  13. Combine the jelly spread side with the peanut butter spread side
  14. Place everything back where it came from
  15. Enjoy your little taste of humanity's greatest
