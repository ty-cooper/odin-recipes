Chicken Salad

Description

If you were to get caught in an alleyway with sketchy figures quickly approaching.. This is what you would want to find in your back pocket. Chicken salad is a wave of relief when re-discovered; Oh yeah this exists and I have it in my fridge. Cold, crisp, fierce, and crunchy. The ultimate companion whether you're cooking at grandma's or lost in a dark, scary place.

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Mayonnaise
  • Lemon
  • Onion
  • Celery

Assembly

  1. Gather all ingredients together
  2. Start by grabbing two forks
  3. Use the two forks as claws, really
  4. Shred that chicken apart into finely slivered slices
  5. Get a chopping board and chef's knife
  6. Give the lemon, celery, and onion all a good rinse
  7. Start with the celery, dry it and chop it into horseshoe chunks
  8. Set those aside in the bowl with the shredded chicken
  9. Finely dice the onion, think McDonald's people
  10. Combine those with the celery and chicken
  11. Grab the mayonnaise, open the lid
  12. Using a spoon, scoop three large scoops of pure Mayo into the bowl with all the ingredients
  13. Mix it well, but not too well
  14. Use more or less mayo depending on the consistency you're looking for
  15. Lastly, squeeze lemon in for a spritz of acid to really bring out that flavor, baby
  16. Return everything back to where it belongs, and boom
  17. Your new best friend
