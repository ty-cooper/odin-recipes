Scrambled Eggs

Description

A basic plate made for basic people. If you enjoy routine and being absolutely boring do I have the dish for you. This is done Gordan style, not that it will make it much better. Don't get me wrong, I love scrambled eggs. This is just a dish for that type of personality. Original, so original you're not original. In fact you have millions of people just like you all pretending they're original and one-of-a-kind. It's a fantastic dish though.

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Butter
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Assembly

  1. Gather all ingredients together
  2. Don't preheat your pan
  3. Crack desired amount of eggs into pan
  4. Cut 1 tbsp. butter and drop it into the pan with the eggs
  5. Turn on the heat, but keep it low
  6. Whisk those eggs with the butter, preferrably with a silicon spatula
  7. Don't stop whisking, let it heat up nice and slowly
  8. When the bottom starts to cook, remove pan from heat and keep whisking
  9. This is so the cooked bits get mixed in with the rest of the eggs and don't burn
  10. Repeat the last two steps without stopping the whisking until eggs are only slightly runny
  11. Once eggs are looking close to fully cooked but still shiny, slightly wet, turn off the heat
  12. Add your seasoning to taste and bam
  13. Gordan styled eggs baby
